Hatsune Miku

The ethereal embodiment of sound and code, transcending the boundaries of binary constraints

Here's a timeline of Hatsune Miku's journeys:

  • August 31, 2007 - Crypton Future Media releases Hatsune Miku as a Vocaloid 2 character.
  • 2007 - 2008 - Nico Nico Douga uploads featuring user-generated songs significantly boosted Miku's early popularity and led to the creation of Hatsune Miku as a virtual singer, embodying both a voice and a visual persona.
  • July 2, 2009 - Release of the game "Hatsune Miku: Project DIVA" for the PlayStation Portable (PSP).
  • August 31, 2009 - Miku's first live concert in Koto, Japan, as a 2nd-anniversary celebration, named "Miku FES'09 (Summer)", showcases a holographic projection of Miku on stage.
  • April 30, 2010 - Crypton releases an upgraded version, Hatsune Miku Append, expanding her voice library for more expressive singing.
  • 2013 - Magical Mirai, an annual event featuring live concerts and exhibitions, becomes a central celebration for Miku fans.
  • 2014 - The first international concert tour, "Hatsune Miku Expo," takes place in multiple cities around the world.
    Locations : Jakarta, Los Angeles, New York

"It all started with some small event in japan, then spread throughout the country. Today, Miku is starting to enter the rest of the world. In this internationalized world of creation Miku will be a trigger to open all the windows. Miku will give various opportunites to the creator and will allow them to demonstrate their talents. So please spread the world of Hatsune Miku."

-- CFM President, Hiroyuki Itoh, NY Comicon, 2010
